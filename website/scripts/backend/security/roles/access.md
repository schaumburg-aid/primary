## Access Guide

The following document contains all permissions for levels, and what the levels give.

### Levels
 > **Level 1** - ***Trial Tester, Tester, Senior Tester, Beta Tester.***
 >> This level has basic permissions, such as the Verified Tag, access to unreleased content, as well as the Level 1 CLI (flagging accounts.).

 > **Level 2** - ***Tester Manger, Trial Moderator, Junior Moderator, Moderator, Safety Council, Support Team.***
 >> This level has basic moderation permissions and are supposed to enforce the rules and regulations of the website. They have the Level 2-3 CLI (suspending (temporary-permanent), flagging, responding to support tickets (only if they are Support Team or Moderator+, mostly Support Team),) as well as all Level 1 Permissions.

 > **Level 3** - ***Senior Moderator, Head Moderator, Support Team Manager, Moderator Manager, Safety Council Manager, Trial Administrator.***
 >> This level has advanced moderation permissions and are supposed to enfore the rules and regulations of the website, while dealing with more serious matters concerning the well being of **users**. They share the same permissions as Level 2 CLI

 > **Level 4** - ***Junior Administrator, Administrator, Senior Administrator, Head Administrator, Staff Manager.***
 >> This level has the most advanced moderation permissions and are supposed to ensure the lower levels are doing their jobs, as well as enforcing the regulations. They have the Level 4 CLI (IP Suspending, MAC Suspending, Site Temporary Shutdown, Account Deletion, all perms from levels before.)/

 > **Level 5** - ***Administrator Manager, Developer, Lead Developer, Site Manager.***
 >> This level has the most permissions and has the power to control the **site** directly. They have the Level 5 CLI (Developer's Console, Codebase Access, Role Management Panel, Site-Wide Announcement, all permissions before.)

 > **Level 6** - ***Creator.***
 >> Every Power.
